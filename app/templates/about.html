Bamnit is a service for substitute teachers to receive notifications when a job they are looking for becomes available.

I created this website for two reasons:

  1. I do part-time work as a substitute teacher, and wanted to get quick emails whenever jobs with my favorite grade or subject to teach were available.
  2. My longer-term career is in computer programming, and I built this project as a demonstration of the variety of my skill set. It took several different pieces of technology to bring everything together, and I'm happy with how it turned out.

Many school districts use a CMS called AesopOnline, which is essentially just a static webpage that lists a school district's available jobs, and which substitute teachers must periodically log onto to check for jobs. Bamnit is a tool where teachers can specify exactly what kind of parameters of a job they are looking for (e.g. Middle School Art classes) and receive an email notification when a job with those parameters is available.

Feel free to create an account, add some notifications, and play around with the tool!
